First Brigade Actions

Pre 1920
Apr 20, 1919 Arms captured in raidon Araglin RIC Barracks by Fermoy Battalion
Sep 7, 1919 Arms capture at Fermoy by Cork No. 2 brigade. One British soldier killed and three wounded.

1920
Apr 3, 1920 Freemount RIC Barracks destroyed in attack.
Jun 20, 1920 Brigadier General Lucas, Commander of 16th. Brigade captured by IRA.
Aug 4, 1920 Kildorrery ambush - at least one Black and Tan killed and 6 wounded
Aug 7 1920 Formation of Second Brigade IRA Flying Column.
Aug 14, 1920 Attack on aeroplane guard at Dromagh, near Clonbanin. One British soldier killed.
Aug 15, 1920 Shootings at Derrygallon, Kanturk. Volunteers Paddy Clancy and Jack O'Connell killed.
Sep 28, 1920 Capture of Mallow military barracks.
Sep 28, 1920 Ballydrochane (Kanturk) Ambush - a number of British killed and wounded.
Oct 17, 1920 Michael Fitzgerald of Fermoy dies on hunger strike at Cork gaol.
Nov 22, 1920 IRA Captain Patrick McCarthy is shot dead during ambush on Black and Tansat Millstreet.
Nov 26, 1920 Three British soldiers killed in ambush near Glanworth.
Dec 1, 1920 Sacking of Fermoy by Auxiliary forces.
Dec 10, 1920 Ambush on military lorry by Fermoy Battalion column- nine British soldiers are disarmed.
Jan 4, 1920 Ambush of British lorries at Meelin, near Newmarket.
Jan 6, 1920 Burning of Meelin village by Black and Tans.
Jan 28, 1920 Ambush at Tureengarriffe, near Kerry-Cork border.
Feb 1, 1920 Four civilians shot dead at Mallow railway station.
Feb 2 1920 Con Murphyfrom near Millstreetis executed by British authorities.
Feb 11 1920 Mallow - Tralee train ambushed at Drishanebeg by Millstreet battalion.
Feb 12, 1920 RIC Constable Patrick Joseph Walsh shot dead at Charleville.
Feb 15, 1920 IRA suffer heavy losses at Mourne Abbey when surprised by British forces.
Mar 5, 1920 British suffer heavy losses atClonbanin Ambush.
Mar 8 1920 Ambush at Fr. Murphy's Bridge, near Banteer by members of Kanturk flying column.
Mar 10 1920 Roundup by British military at Nadd, North Cork. FourIRA volunteers are shot dead.
Mar 13, 1920 Vice-Commandant Charles O'Reilly shot dead by Black and Tans at Coolagh Bridge, Newmarket.
Mar 18, 1920 RIC patrol ambushed near Castletownroche.
Apr 10 1920 2 RIC constables shot dead at Scart, near Kildorrery.
Apr 25, 1920 British intelligence officer executed by I.R.A. near Charleville
May 15, 1920 Arrest of Commandant Sean Moylan by British at Kiskeam

1921
Jun 1, 1921 RIC member Joseph Holman shot dead near Kilworth.
Jun 16, 1921 Overtwenty auxiliaries killed or wounded inambush at Rathcoole, near Millstreet.
Jun 27 1921 RIC member Thomas Shanley shot dead at Kildorrery.
